j - mode the cursor down
k - move the cursor up
w - move to the beginning of the next word
b - move back to the beginning of the word


yy - yank(copy) the line
p  - paste the line
dd - delete the line
u  - will undo the last change

shift + v - will highlight a whole line
    - use j,k,h,l to move and select more words/lines
    - use y - it will copy the whole selection
    - use d - and it will delete the whole selection

dw - it delete the word to the end
dj - it deletes the current line and plus one down

yw - it copy the word to the end
yj - it copy the current line and plus one down
